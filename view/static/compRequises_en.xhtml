 What does a shipmate is supposed to do ?

It depends on what the skipper expects. What the shipmate has to do has to be defined before going on board !

Usually, the shipmate has to participate to navigation, to steerages, to help to clean the boat, and sometimes to cook and to do the washing-up.
Maybe you will also help to repair various stuffs, to give advice about computer sciences, or to be a translator in marinas.

 How much does it cost to be a boat-hitchhiker ?

It's linked to the previous point what a shipmate is supposed to do. The price depends on the skipper.

Usually, you have at least to participate to a common till aimed at buying food (around 5€ per day), or if not, you have to bring your own food and water. Some boats' owners also add the fuel and the marinas' fees to the common till (which is about 25 € per day).

There are also some specific cases, such as yacht delivery. In that case, you have the possibility to earn a few money, or to have food and drinking water for free.

As you've already understood, many points depend on the captain; this is why you have to define with him the conditions before leaving. Then you'll be free to decide which proposal suits you the most.

But please remember: the human interactions are the most important part of a stay on Sea!

 Which qualities are important to find a boat ?

A sailboat is tiny, this is why cohabiting with other shipmates is very important.

Thus, the first criterion the captain will take into account (even before your skills) is your manners and the way you behave.

You have therefore to be pleasant and in a good mood. You'll also have to be able to get along with people you didn't know before during several days (or weeks, or months), even if the life conditions are sometimes difficult.

This is why we advise you to complete your profile and your as much as possible. Show that you're really motivated, that you're able to adapt to any situation, and most of all that you really wish to learn and to share a great experience.

 Except www.bateaustop.fr, are there other ways to find a boat ?

On bateaustop.fr, you have the possibility to publish requests. Nevertheless, you also have the possibility to find a boat directly in place. Marinas are places which helps a lot in finding contacts and access to boats. If you're interested in a boat lied at anchor, you have to use a small zodiac (or something similar) in order to meet the owners and speak to them.

In case of direct contact, it's better to have something to present. For instance, a map of the travel you wish to do, or photos of your voyage. This is a nice way to start a talk.
